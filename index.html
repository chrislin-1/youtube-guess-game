How to Play

Guess how many views today's video has on YouTube.

With every guess, you'll learn how close you are to the actual answer, as well as if you're too high or too low.

Example Game:

  • Your guess (50,000,000) is considered far because its more than 30% away from the correct answer. Your answer is also too low, so the finger icon points up.

    50,000,000 - You're way off...

  • Your guess (100,000,000) is considered close because its within 30% away from the correct answer. Your answer is now too high, so the finger icon points down.

    100,000,000 - You're close...

  • Your guess (90,000,000) was within 5% of the answer (92,254,496) You Win!

    Correct! 92,254,496 views
